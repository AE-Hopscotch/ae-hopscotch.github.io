Concept Projects

Concept Projects are proof of concept demos that show particular uses for certain modded features. These are based around features that I've added into my modded player, and they demonstrate the capability as well as the feasability of inplementing them into Hopscotch. If I can implement a feature that I've wanted to see in Hopscotch and show a proof of concept demonstrating its purpose, then what reason is there to not implement it natively in vanilla Hopscotch?
